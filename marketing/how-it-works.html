How It Works

1. Unlock Your Claim

Purchase access to one complete claim toolkit. One-time payment, no monthly fees.

2. Set Up Your Claim

Complete a quick onboarding wizard to provide your claim details, property information, and loss description.

3. Follow the Roadmap

Use the guided Claim Roadmap to move through each stage of your claim with clear next steps and state-specific guidance.

4. Generate Documents

Create professional claim documents using our Document Generator. All documents are personalized with your claim data.

5. Organize Evidence

Upload photos and organize evidence with our Evidence Organizer. AI-powered categorization makes it simple.

6. Respond to Insurer

Use the AI Response Agent to draft professional responses to insurer communications. State-aware and compliant.

7. Track Your Progress

Monitor your Claim Health Score and activation progress. Know exactly what needs attention.
