Frequently Asked Questions

How does pricing work?

We charge $99 per claim. One payment unlocks all tools for one complete claim. No monthly fees, no hidden costs.

What tools are included?

You get access to the Document Generator, AI Response Agent, Evidence Organizer, Claim Health Engine, Claim Portfolio Generator, and the complete Claim Roadmap with state-specific guidance.

Is this legal advice?

No. Claim Navigator provides informational tools and document assistance only. It is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice. For legal questions, consult a licensed attorney.

How long do I have access?

You have access to your claim tools for as long as you need to resolve your claim. There are no time limits.

Can I use this for multiple claims?

Each purchase unlocks one claim. If you have multiple claims, you'll need to purchase access for each one separately.
