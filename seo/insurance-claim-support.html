Why Claims Require Support

Filing an insurance claim after property damage should be straightforward, but the process is rarely simple. Claims get delayed for unclear reasons. Adjusters provide estimates that don't match contractor quotes. You need support navigating a system that seems designed to frustrate rather than help.

Information Asymmetry

Insurance companies know exactly how claims are evaluated, what documentation is required, and which arguments succeed. Policyholders don't. This knowledge gap means you're negotiating blind.

Process Complexity

The claim process involves multiple steps with strict deadlines and specific requirements. Miss a deadline or submit incomplete documentation, and your claim can be denied or reduced.

Technical Evaluation

Adjusters use specialized software and pricing databases to evaluate damage. Without technical support, you can't effectively challenge these evaluations.

Communication Barriers

Insurance adjusters speak a specialized language full of industry terms and policy references. Effective communication requires understanding this language.
