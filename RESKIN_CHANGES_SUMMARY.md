# Claim Navigator Landing Page Reskin - Summary

## Date: January 5, 2026

## Objective
Reskin the Claim Navigator landing page to match a conservative insurance/legal industry aesthetic while preserving all existing copy, sections, and functionality.

## Changes Implemented

### 1. Hero Section ✅
- **Background**: Changed from dark overlay to clean navy-to-blue gradient (`#0B2545` → `#123A63`)
- **Layout**: Converted from centered single-column to horizontal two-column layout
  - Left column: Product name + subtitle
  - Right column: Description + primary CTA
- **Typography**: 
  - Text aligned left instead of center
  - Maintained white/light gray text for contrast against dark blue
  - Font weight reduced from 800 to 700 for more professional look
- **Padding**: Increased vertical padding for breathing room

### 2. Primary CTA Buttons ✅
- **Color**: Changed from blue gradient to teal (`#17BEBB`)
- **Hover State**: Subtle darken to `#13a09d` (no animations)
- **Border Radius**: Reduced to 5px for cleaner look
- **Shadows**: Removed all box shadows

### 3. Typography & Contrast ✅
- **Hero Text**: White (`#ffffff`) and light gray (`#e8eef5`) on dark blue background
- **Body Text**: Dark gray (`#1f2937` and `#4b5563`) on white/light backgrounds
- **Headings**: Consistent dark gray (`#1f2937`) throughout content sections
- **Links**: Gray (`#4b5563`) with teal hover (`#17BEBB`)

### 4. Content Sections ✅
- **Background Colors**: 
  - Alternating white (`#ffffff`) and light gray (`#F7F9FC`)
  - Removed all dark blue/navy backgrounds
- **Spacing**: Increased vertical spacing between sections
- **Visual Noise**: 
  - Removed all card shadows
  - Removed decorative borders and glows
  - Simplified to clean borders (`#e5e7eb`)

### 5. Navigation Header ✅
- **Background**: Changed from dark navy to white
- **Logo**: Changed from blue to navy (`#0B2545`)
- **Links**: Changed from white to gray (`#4b5563`)
- **Border**: Added subtle bottom border (`#e5e7eb`)
- **Shadow**: Reduced to minimal shadow for depth

### 6. Component Updates ✅

#### Tool Cards

### 7. Footer ✅
- **Background**: Light gray (`#F7F9FC`)

### 8. Floating/Sticky CTAs ✅
- **Background**: Teal (`#17BEBB`)

- All blue accent colors (replaced with teal)

## Design Principles Applied

1. **Conservative & Professional**: Clean, document-like layout similar to established insurance/legal sites
2. **High Contrast**: Dark text on light backgrounds for readability
3. **Minimal Decoration**: No shadows, glows, or visual effects
4. **Consistent Spacing**: Generous whitespace and breathing room
5. **Flat Design**: No depth effects or layering
6. **Authoritative Color Palette**: Navy, teal, grays, and white
7. **Clean Typography**: Professional font weights and sizes

## Technical Notes

- No copy or wording changed
- No sections added, removed, or reordered
- No icons, images, or illustrations added
- All existing functionality preserved
- Mobile responsive layouts maintained
- Bilingual support (English/Spanish) preserved
- All CTAs and links functional

## Result

The landing page now presents a calm, authoritative, and established aesthetic appropriate for the insurance/legal industry, without appearing "startup" or "AI-styled." The design emphasizes clarity, professionalism, and trustworthiness.
