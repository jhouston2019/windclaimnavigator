How to Use This Site

A simple 4-step system to help you organize, document, and communicate your claim the way insurers require.

The 4-Step System

Move in order: describe your situation, generate the right documents, analyze and compare what you receive, then track every milestone.

  1. Describe Your Situation: Capture facts once and guide every tool.
  2. Generate Documents: Create letters and forms that match insurer requirements.
  3. Analyze & Compare: Review coverage, estimates, and offers before responding.
  4. Track Progress: Deadlines, stages, journal entries, and timelines in one place.

Recommended Order of Use

  • Input your situation
  • Upload your evidence
  • Generate essential documents
  • Compare estimates
  • Track deadlines
  • Follow Claim Playbook during disputes
  • Close your claim

Key Tools You Must Use

  • AI Response Agent: Reads insurer letters, spots deadlines, and drafts responses.
  • Document Generator: Produces insurer-ready letters and forms from guided prompts.
  • Evidence Organizer: Keeps proofs tagged and linked so every submission is supported.
  • Deadlines Tool: Prevents missed response dates with reminders and calendar clarity.
  • Claim Stage Tracker: Shows current stage, required tasks, and what to do next.

How the AI Agent Works

  • Analyzes insurer letters to extract issues and requests.
  • Detects response timelines and proof-of-loss deadlines.
  • Generates recommended responses with citations and next actions.
  • Syncs outputs into the Claim Journal for full history.

How to Track Your Claim

  • Stage tracker workflow shows where you are and what is due.
  • Timeline progression maps every milestone and response.
  • Journal syncing keeps notes, uploads, and letters together.
  • Evidence linking ties proofs to documents, deadlines, and stages.
