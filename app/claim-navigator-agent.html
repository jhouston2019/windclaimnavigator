What the Agent Does

The Claim Navigator Agent continuously ingests insurer communications, extracts obligations, and updates your workflows. It keeps every deadline, follow-up, and requirement synced across the claim so the team stays ahead without extra effort.

  • Detects deadlines from insurer communications
  • Generates automatic follow-ups
  • Monitors carrier compliance
  • Syncs Statement of Loss data
  • Tracks documentation requirements
  • Maintains communication timeline
  • Flags coverage issues
