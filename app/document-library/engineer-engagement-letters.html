Engineer Engagement Letters

Engage engineers with precise questions and safety expectations.

Scope Timeline Deliverables

Overview

Purpose

  • Define property details, loss background, and questions to answer.
  • Clarify independence, reporting format, and delivery dates.
  • Note site access rules and contacts.

Quality checklist

Ready-to-send

  • Include claim identifiers and location details.
  • Outline safety constraints and chain-of-custody needs.
  • Attach any prior reports to avoid duplication.
  • Confirm fee terms and invoicing instructions.

What to include

Use these pieces to keep the engineer engagement letters packet complete.

Core documents

Build the file

  • Engagement letter with scope of work.
  • Schedule and access instructions.
  • Fee schedule or rate sheet.

Supporting proof

Evidence to attach

  • Plans, photos, or sketches for pre-review.
  • Known damage locations to prioritize.
  • Prior reports or estimates for context.

Delivery notes

Keep it moving

  • Send via email with required response date.
  • Confirm insurance requirements and certificates.
  • Capture acceptance signature or email confirmation.
