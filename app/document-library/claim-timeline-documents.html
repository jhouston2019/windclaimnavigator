Overview

Purpose

  • Outline key dates from loss, notice, inspections, payments, and negotiations.
  • Pair dates with references to documents or communications.
  • Highlight statutory or policy-driven deadlines.

Quality checklist

Ready-to-send

  • Verify dates against policy conditions and correspondence.
  • Include time-stamped evidence where available.
  • Note pending actions with owners and target dates.

What to include

Use these pieces to keep the claim timeline documents packet complete.

Core documents

Build the file

  • Master timeline spreadsheet or tracker.
  • Milestone summary memo with key dates.
  • Deadline tracker with reminders and owners.

Supporting proof

Evidence to attach

  • Receipts or confirmations of submissions.
  • Emails confirming inspections or meetings.
  • Calendar invites and attendance notes.

Delivery notes

Keep it moving

  • Update weekly and share with the claim team.
  • Provide a PDF copy when disputes arise.
  • Keep versions to show when new data was added.
