Settlement Closure Documentation

Overview

Purpose

  • Restate settlement amounts and coverage buckets.
  • Note conditions, releases, and timelines.
  • Outline next steps for payment and closing the claim.

Quality checklist

Ready-to-send

  • Confirm who will sign and their authority.
  • List contingencies, holdbacks, or conditions.
  • Provide wiring or payment instructions.
  • Set expectations for release and payment dates.

What to include

Use these pieces to keep the settlement and negotiation letters packet complete.

Core documents

Build the file

  • Settlement confirmation letter.
  • Release draft or agreement.
  • Payment instruction sheet.

Supporting proof

Evidence to attach

  • Payment ledger or summary.
  • Tax forms such as W-9 if needed.
  • Proof of authority to sign for the insured or entity.

Delivery notes

Keep it moving

  • Send for signature using e-sign or certified mail.
  • Request payment remittance detail and timeline.
  • Track issuance and receipt of funds.
