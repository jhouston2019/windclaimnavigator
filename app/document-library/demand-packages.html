Demand Packages

Present a clear demand with evidence, math, and requested response.

Negotiation Evidence Valuation

Overview

Purpose

  • Use when requesting payment or settlement after presenting evidence.
  • Summarize claim posture, coverage positions, and valuation.
  • Include an exhibit index that mirrors the demand narrative.

Quality checklist

Ready-to-send

  • State the demand amount and calculation basis.
  • Address carrier positions or prior offers.
  • Verify math ties to exhibits and estimates.
  • Provide a response deadline and delivery instructions.

What to include

Use these pieces to keep the demand packages packet complete.

Core documents

Build the file

  • Demand letter with executive summary.
  • Exhibit list with numbering and labels.
  • Valuation summary by coverage bucket.

Supporting proof

Evidence to attach

  • Invoices, estimates, or BI calculations.
  • Expert reports or engineer findings.
  • Photos, logs, and correspondence referenced in the letter.

Delivery notes

Keep it moving

  • Combine as a bookmarked PDF or secure link.
  • Note file sizes and provide alternate delivery if needed.
  • Request written acknowledgement and set a response date.
