Environmental Health Remediation

Overview

Purpose

  • Document moisture sources, affected areas, and spread.
  • Include lab results, protocols, and clearance targets.
  • Address occupant impacts and safety guidance.

Quality checklist

Ready-to-send

  • Maintain chain-of-custody for samples.
  • Provide pre- and post-remediation testing results.
  • Label photos with locations and dates.

What to include

Use these pieces to keep the mold claim documents packet complete.

Core documents

Build the file

  • Industrial hygienist or mold assessor report.
  • Moisture mapping and readings.
  • Remediation protocol and scope.

Supporting proof

Evidence to attach

  • Lab results and clearance certificates.
  • HVAC or ventilation assessments.
  • Tenant or occupant notices and instructions.

Delivery notes

Keep it moving

  • Store certifications separately for easy sharing.
  • Provide PPE and safety notes for site visits.
  • Coordinate re-occupancy clearance with stakeholders.
