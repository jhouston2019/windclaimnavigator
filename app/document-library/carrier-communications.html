Carrier Communications

Maintain a reliable record of who said what, when, and what was promised.

Traceability Deadlines Audit trail

Overview

Purpose

  • Keep a single log for calls, emails, portal posts, and letters.
  • Capture participants, topics, commitments, and due dates.
  • Link each entry to related documents or tasks.

Quality checklist

Ready-to-send

  • Update the log in real time or immediately after conversations.
  • Note promised deliverables, owners, and follow-up dates.
  • Store copies of messages and attachments with each entry.

What to include

Use these pieces to keep the carrier communications packet complete.

Core documents

Build the file

  • Communication log spreadsheet or form.
  • Call recap notes with time stamps.
  • Portal submission confirmations and message PDFs.

Supporting proof

Evidence to attach

  • Email threads or letter PDFs.
  • Screenshots of portal activity.
  • Calendar invites for scheduled meetings or inspections.

Delivery notes

Keep it moving

  • Share log excerpts with the team weekly.
  • Export the log for mediation or regulatory responses.
  • Back up the log to the claim file with versioning.
