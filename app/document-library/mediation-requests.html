Mediation Requests

Frame the dispute, parties, and desired outcomes for mediation.

ADR Deadlines Positions

Overview

Purpose

  • Summarize dispute issues and sought outcomes.
  • Provide brief chronology and current negotiation status.
  • List parties, representatives, and authority levels.

Quality checklist

Ready-to-send

  • Include mediator preferences and availability windows.
  • Attach key exhibits and current demands or offers.
  • Identify participants with settlement authority.

What to include

Use these pieces to keep the mediation requests packet complete.

Core documents

Build the file

  • Mediation request letter.
  • Position statement or brief.
  • Exhibit packet supporting the issues.

Supporting proof

Evidence to attach

  • History of settlement offers.
  • Critical correspondence or denials.
  • Valuation summaries or expert highlights.

Delivery notes

Keep it moving

  • Coordinate dates with carrier and counsel.
  • Provide conference logistics and platform details.
  • Confirm confidentiality and mediation agreements.
