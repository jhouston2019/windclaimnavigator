Evidence Documents

Organize proofs with clear labels and traceability.

Proof Organized Traceable

Overview

Purpose

  • Maintain a dedicated evidence folder with clear naming.
  • Label each file with date, source, and relevance.
  • Map evidence to claim issues and policy provisions.

Quality checklist

Ready-to-send

  • Preserve original metadata when possible.
  • Separate drafts from final documents.
  • Document chain-of-custody for sensitive items.

What to include

Use these pieces to keep the evidence documents packet complete.

Core documents

Build the file

  • Evidence index with references.
  • Witness statements or affidavits.
  • Testing or inspection reports.

Supporting proof

Evidence to attach

  • Photos and videos with captions.
  • Receipts and invoices tied to line items.
  • Correspondence confirming facts or approvals.

Delivery notes

Keep it moving

  • Store in a read-only share for external recipients.
  • Include a legend for abbreviations or coding.
  • Back up locally and in the cloud.
