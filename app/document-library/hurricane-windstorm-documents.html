Weather Roof Mitigation

Overview

Purpose

  • Document storm timing, wind speeds, and site conditions.
  • Show roof and exterior impacts with location tags.
  • Track temporary repairs and tarping efforts.

Quality checklist

Ready-to-send

  • Capture NOAA or weather service data for the loss period.
  • Include photos or drone imagery by elevation and slope.
  • Attach invoices for debris removal and temporary protection.

What to include

Use these pieces to keep the hurricane & windstorm documents packet complete.

Core documents

Build the file

  • Weather reports and radar summaries.
  • Roof or building inspection reports.
  • Mitigation and tarping receipts.

Supporting proof

Evidence to attach

  • Drone images with annotations.
  • Engineer notes on causation and scope.
  • Moisture mapping or interior leak tracking.

Delivery notes

Keep it moving

  • Organize by building and elevation.
  • Note safety or access limits for inspectors.
  • Provide a storm timeline with gusts and outages.
