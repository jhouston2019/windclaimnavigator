Perishables Operations Health

Overview

Purpose

  • Document spoilage, closures, and seating or capacity changes.
  • Track lost revenue and extra expenses specific to service model.
  • Include health or safety compliance steps.

Quality checklist

Ready-to-send

  • Health department notices or permit updates.
  • Inventory of spoiled goods with photos and counts.
  • Payroll or staffing impacts and mitigation actions.

What to include

Use these pieces to keep the restaurant loss documentation packet complete.

Core documents

Build the file

  • Spoilage logs with item details.
  • Seating/capacity charts or floorplans.
  • Business interruption calculations for restaurant operations.

Supporting proof

Evidence to attach

  • POS reports before and after the loss.
  • Supplier cancellations or delay notices.
  • Sanitation and cleaning reports.

Delivery notes

Keep it moving

  • Highlight time-sensitive perishables in the cover memo.
  • Bundle with BI and expense trackers.
  • Coordinate reopening updates with landlord and insurer.
