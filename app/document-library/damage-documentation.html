Damage Documentation

Pair visuals and scope notes with estimates and readings.

Evidence Scope Photos

Overview

Purpose

  • Document damages room-by-room with clear identifiers.
  • Pair photos with notes, locations, and line-item references.
  • Tie observations to estimates or scopes per trade.

Quality checklist

Ready-to-send

  • Use date-stamped photos and videos.
  • Cross-reference to room or line numbers in the estimate.
  • Capture pre-loss condition where available.

What to include

Use these pieces to keep the damage documentation packet complete.

Core documents

Build the file

  • Room-by-room damage logs.
  • Annotated photo sheets.
  • Scope summaries for each trade.

Supporting proof

Evidence to attach

  • Contractor or Xactimate estimates.
  • Moisture or thermal readings with locations.
  • Temporary repair receipts and notes.

Delivery notes

Keep it moving

  • Organize by area or trade in labeled folders.
  • Compress media without losing clarity.
  • Include a cover sheet summarizing findings.
