Legal Documents

Track filings, service, and signed agreements with discipline.

Counsel Deadlines Compliance

Overview

Purpose

  • Store pleadings, agreements, and legal notices by case or matter.
  • Track filing deadlines and hearing dates.
  • Retain proof of service and signatures.

Quality checklist

Ready-to-send

  • Verify venue, caption, and case numbers are correct.
  • Redact sensitive data per rules or orders.
  • Keep signed versions and drafts separate.
  • Log filing confirmations and service dates.

What to include

Use these pieces to keep the legal documents packet complete.

Core documents

Build the file

  • Retainer or engagement agreements.
  • Pleadings, complaints, and motions.
  • Affidavits, declarations, and exhibits.

Supporting proof

Evidence to attach

  • Service affidavits and mailing receipts.
  • E-filing or court receipt confirmations.
  • Settlement agreement drafts and notes.

Delivery notes

Keep it moving

  • Name files consistently with dates and docket numbers.
  • Maintain version history for each draft.
  • Store in a secure location with permissions.
