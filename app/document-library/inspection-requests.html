Inspection Requests

Give inspectors what they need to arrive prepared and focused.

Scheduling Access Prep

Overview

Purpose

  • State the purpose and scope of the inspection.
  • Provide site access details, contacts, and safety requirements.
  • List documents or areas to prioritize.

Quality checklist

Ready-to-send

  • Confirm date and time with all parties.
  • Prepare documents and prior reports onsite.
  • Record attendee names and initial findings.

What to include

Use these pieces to keep the inspection requests packet complete.

Core documents

Build the file

  • Inspection request letter or email.
  • Agenda or scope outline.
  • Access instructions and maps.

Supporting proof

Evidence to attach

  • Photos highlighting focus areas.
  • Prior estimates or reports.
  • Security, parking, or safety notes.

Delivery notes

Keep it moving

  • Send confirmation with calendar invite.
  • Capture sign-in or attendance sheets.
  • Share a recap within 24 hours of the visit.
