Core Documents

Foundation Basics Identifiers

Overview

Purpose

  • Keep policy, claim identifiers, and contact details together.
  • Document loss description, date, and initial facts.
  • Store representation or authorization letters where applicable.

Quality checklist

Ready-to-send

  • Policy declarations and endorsements are current.
  • Claim number, insured contact, and loss location are consistent.
  • Authority letters or POA are signed and filed.
  • Contact tree for carrier, vendors, and insured is updated.

What to include

Use these pieces to keep the core documents packet complete.

Core documents

Build the file

  • Policy declarations and endorsements.
  • Claim intake form and loss summary.
  • Letter of representation or authorization.

Supporting proof

Evidence to attach

  • Photo overview of the loss site.
  • ID verification or POA where needed.
  • Adjuster contact sheet with roles.

Delivery notes

Keep it moving

  • Store in a clearly named root folder.
  • Share with vendors as a starter packet.
  • Version files with dates to avoid confusion.
