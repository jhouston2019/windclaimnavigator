Financial Documents

Provide verified financials with clear explanations and protections.

Accounting Verification Support

Overview

Purpose

  • Identify which financial statements the claim needs.
  • Ensure statements are signed or certified when appropriate.
  • Highlight relevant periods and any unusual adjustments.

Quality checklist

Ready-to-send

  • Include profit and loss, balance sheet, and cash flows as needed.
  • Explain normalizations or add-backs in plain language.
  • Protect personally identifiable information with redactions.
  • Coordinate with accounting or CPA for accuracy.

What to include

Use these pieces to keep the financial documents packet complete.

Core documents

Build the file

  • Profit and loss statements for relevant periods.
  • Balance sheet and cash flow statements.
  • Tax return excerpts that support the claim.

Supporting proof

Evidence to attach

  • General ledger exports tied to calculations.
  • Bank statements or deposits evidence.
  • CPA letters or attestation notes.

Delivery notes

Keep it moving

  • Provide a summary memo linking figures to exhibits.
  • Include an index for quick navigation.
  • Encrypt or password-protect when sending externally.
