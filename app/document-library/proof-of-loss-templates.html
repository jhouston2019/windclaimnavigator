Overview

Purpose

  • Use the correct form and policy-required wording.
  • Show valuation math and how amounts were derived.
  • Note submission requirements and delivery methods.

Quality checklist

Ready-to-send

  • Include notarization where required by the carrier.
  • Match amounts to estimates and exhibits.
  • Meet submission deadlines and retain proof of delivery.

What to include

Use these pieces to keep the proof of loss templates packet complete.

Core documents

Build the file

  • Sworn proof of loss form.
  • Loss summary schedule by coverage.
  • Notary acknowledgement or jurat as required.

Supporting proof

Evidence to attach

  • Estimate printouts and summaries.
  • Inventory or contents worksheets.
  • Photos and receipts backing claimed amounts.

Delivery notes

Keep it moving

  • Send via the carriers required channel (portal or mail).
  • Save delivery receipts and confirmation numbers.
  • Retain signed originals securely.
