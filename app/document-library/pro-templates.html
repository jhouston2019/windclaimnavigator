Overview

Purpose

  • Centralize branded templates for recurring communications.
  • Maintain compliant language and accessibility-friendly formatting.
  • Provide both editable and PDF-ready versions.

Quality checklist

Ready-to-send

  • Include logo, contact details, and disclaimers.
  • Offer plain language options where appropriate.
  • Version templates with dates and owners.
  • Note when to customize versus keep standard.

What to include

Use these pieces to keep the pro templates packet complete.

Core documents

Build the file

  • Letterhead and cover templates.
  • Statement of loss and summary templates.
  • General notice and acknowledgement templates.

Supporting proof

Evidence to attach

  • Style guide and tone references.
  • Signature blocks for the team.
  • Usage instructions and approval steps.

Delivery notes

Keep it moving

  • Store master files read-only; distribute copies for use.
  • Provide fillable PDFs alongside editable originals.
  • Retire outdated versions promptly.
