Document stolen or damaged property with proof and reports.

Overview

Purpose

  • Detail stolen or damaged items with values and identifiers.
  • Provide police reports and incident numbers.
  • Show security measures and evidence collected.

Quality checklist

Ready-to-send

  • Include serial numbers, model info, and purchase dates.
  • Document forced entry or damage with photos.
  • Record discovery time and reporting timeline.

What to include

Use these pieces to keep the vandalism and theft documents packet complete.

Core documents

Build the file

  • Police or incident report.
  • Inventory of stolen or damaged property.
  • Security footage summary or stills.

Supporting proof

Evidence to attach

  • Photos or video stills of the scene.
  • Purchase receipts or warranties.
  • Alarm logs or access control records.

Delivery notes

Keep it moving

  • Submit promptly with report numbers.
  • Preserve evidence and chain of custody.
  • Coordinate follow-up with law enforcement and carrier.
