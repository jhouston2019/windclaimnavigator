First Notice of Loss

Submit accurate claim intake details with immediate mitigation steps.

Intake Basics Speed

Overview

Purpose

  • Gather loss date, cause, location, and contact information.
  • Capture immediate mitigation taken and current site condition.
  • Confirm coverage information and any hazards on site.

Quality checklist

Ready-to-send

  • Accurate date, time, and description of loss.
  • Policy number and insured contact details.
  • Access instructions and safety considerations.
  • Photos or reports ready for submission.

What to include

Use these pieces to keep the first notice of loss packet complete.

Core documents

Build the file

  • FNOL form or intake sheet.
  • Loss summary statement.
  • Immediate mitigation receipts or invoices.

Supporting proof

Evidence to attach

  • Initial photos or videos.
  • Police or fire reports if applicable.
  • Temporary repair invoices and notes.

Delivery notes

Keep it moving

  • Submit via carrier portal or phone and note confirmation.
  • Record the assigned claim number and adjuster.
  • Log the date/time of filing in the timeline tracker.
