How to use this ROM

A ROM (Rough Order of Magnitude) gives a directional cost range before detailed scoping or contractor bids are available. It is not a final estimate, but a fast way to gauge budget and spot obvious gaps in a carrier’s number.

Use it to set expectations, prioritize inspections, and decide which areas need deeper documentation before negotiations start.

Best for

  • Quick budgeting
  • Cross-checking carrier estimates
  • Understanding cost ranges
  • Highlighting discrepancies
  • Supporting supplement requests
