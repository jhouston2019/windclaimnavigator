Why vetted resources matter

When to use professionals

Structural issues, engineering questions, complex valuations, coverage disputes, code upgrades, or when an insurer challenges your scope.

When DIY is enough

Basic photo evidence, simple receipts, organizing correspondence, and capturing before/after visuals you can validate later.

Benefits of trusted resources

  • Faster documentation
  • More accurate estimates
  • Legal & technical expertise
  • Correct valuation
  • Reduced delays
