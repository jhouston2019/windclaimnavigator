Why State Insurance Departments Matter

State Departments of Insurance (DOIs) regulate carriers, enforce consumer protections, and investigate unfair claim practices.

When to Contact Them

Engage your DOI when communication stalls, timelines slip, or carrier conduct appears unfair or non-compliant.

Common Triggers

  • Claim delays
  • Non-response
  • Lowballing
  • Bad faith indicators
  • Unfair settlement practices

When to Contact Your State DOI

Escalate to your Department of Insurance when carrier conduct delays or jeopardizes a fair claim resolution.

  • 30+ days with no response
  • Repeated document requests already delivered
  • Suspicious or inconsistent engineering reports
  • Extreme delays with no clear timeline
  • Bad faith indicators or denied inspections
