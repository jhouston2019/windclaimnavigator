How to Counter Insurance Tactics

Documentation + Evidence

  • Maintain a dated log of every request, submission, and response.
  • Bundle photos, estimates, and receipts with short cover notes.
  • Re-send key packets after adjuster changes to avoid resets.

Timeline Enforcement

  • Cite state acknowledgment and decision deadlines in follow-ups.
  • Set reply-by dates in writing and escalate after missed windows.
  • Use certified email or portal uploads to capture timestamps.

Policy Language

  • Quote exact policy provisions when disputing misstatements.
  • Request carrier citations for any exclusion they invoke.
  • Keep a side-by-side of your duties and completed actions.

Proper Use of the Claim Playbook

  • Use pre-written scripts for delay, denial, and underpayment responses.
  • Pair each script with evidence links and recent photos.
  • Track carrier replies and pivot to the next escalation step.

When to File a DOI Complaint

  • Document repeated missed deadlines or unexplained silence.
  • Attach your communication log and evidence packets.
  • Notify the carrier in writing before filing to prompt resolution.

When to Escalate to Legal Counsel

  • Persistent underpayment after complete evidence is provided.
  • Bad faith indicators such as fabricated exclusions or altered reports.
  • Impending suit limitation deadlines with unresolved disputes.
