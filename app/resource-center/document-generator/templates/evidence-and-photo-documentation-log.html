Evidence And Photo Documentation Log

This template is part of the Claim Navigator Document Generator system.

Purpose

Use this document template to create professional, comprehensive documentation for your insurance claim.

How to Use

  • Review the template structure and required information
  • Gather all necessary documentation and evidence
  • Fill in your specific claim details
  • Review for accuracy and completeness
  • Submit to your insurance carrier with supporting documents

Best Practices

  • Be thorough and specific in all descriptions
  • Include all relevant dates, times, and circumstances
  • Attach supporting documentation (photos, receipts, reports)
  • Keep copies of all submitted materials
  • Follow up with your adjuster within 3-5 business days
