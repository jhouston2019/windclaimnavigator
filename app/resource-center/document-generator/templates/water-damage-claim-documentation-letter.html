Water Damage Claim Documentation Letter

This template helps you create a comprehensive water damage claim documentation letter to submit to your insurance carrier.

Purpose

Use this letter to formally document water damage to your property and provide detailed information about the loss event, damages sustained, and supporting evidence.

What to Include

  • Date and time the water damage was discovered
  • Source of water (burst pipe, roof leak, flooding, etc.)
  • Immediate actions taken to mitigate damage
  • Detailed description of affected areas and items
  • List of damaged property with estimated values
  • Photos and videos of the damage
  • Receipts for emergency repairs or mitigation services
  • Contact information for any contractors or restoration companies

Key Components

  • Loss Details: Specific date, time, and circumstances of the water damage event
  • Affected Areas: Room-by-room breakdown of impacted spaces
  • Property Damage: Itemized list of damaged contents, fixtures, and structural elements
  • Mitigation Efforts: Steps taken to prevent further damage
  • Supporting Documentation: Reference to attached photos, invoices, and reports

Best Practices

  • Submit within 24-48 hours of discovering the damage
  • Be thorough and specific in your descriptions
  • Include all relevant policy information
  • Keep copies of all submitted documentation
  • Follow up with your adjuster within 3-5 business days
