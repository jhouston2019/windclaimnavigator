How to Use This Directory Strategically

  • Step 1: Identify your state or jurisdiction and open the official website.
  • Step 2: Locate the consumer complaint or "File a Complaint" section.
  • Step 3: Use Claim Navigator tools (AI Response Agent, Document Generator, Evidence Vault, Proof of Loss) to prepare evidence before you submit.
  • Step 4: Keep copies of everything you send and log all activity in the Claim Diary tool.

Note: Regulatory information can change. This directory is based on the most recent publicly available regulator data; always confirm details on the official department website.
